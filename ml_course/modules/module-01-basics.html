Supervised vs. Unsupervised

How do machines learn? It depends on whether they have a teacher or if they're exploring on their own.

The Child's Intuition

How do you teach a child to recognize a cat?

Supervised: You show them pictures and say "This is a Cat", "This is a Dog". You provide labels.

Unsupervised: You give them a pile of cards and say "Group these by how they look". They might group by color, size, or shape without knowing the names.
